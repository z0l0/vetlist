# Claim Confirmation Email Template

Use this template when confirming a practice has successfully claimed their listing.

---

**Subject:** Your VetList.org Profile Has Been Updated

**Email Body:**

Hi [Practice Name/Contact Name],

Thank you for claiming your practice on VetList.org! Your profile has been successfully updated.

You can view your updated listing here:  
**[INSERT PROFILE URL]**

If you need to make any changes or have questions about your listing, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Best regards,  
**VetList.org Team**

---

## Quick Fill Instructions

1. Replace `[Practice Name/Contact Name]` with the actual contact name
2. Insert their direct profile URL in the placeholder
3. Send from your VetList.org email address

## Profile URL Format

- Format: `https://vetlist.org/[country]/[region]/[city]/[practice-name-slug]/`
- Example: `https://vetlist.org/canada/ontario/toronto/downtown-veterinary-clinic/`
